Payments Operations

Roku Pay Health of Business Monitoring

Problem: Payment health was spread across multiple critical KPIs and required fast coordination across product, engineering, finance, risk, and external processors.

What I owned: Health of Business monitoring for Roku Pay across authorization, conversion, cancellations, tokenization, purchase errors, CIT/MIT, and COGS.

Outcome: Stronger visibility into payment performance, faster issue detection, and sharper response during day-to-day operations and tentpole events.

AI-Native Operations

LLM-Powered Alert Triage

Problem: Alerts from tools like Grafana, Datadog, and Tableau can be noisy, duplicative, and costly when humans must manually triage everything.

What I built: A workflow to ingest monitoring signals, filter false positives, prioritize real incidents, and support human-in-the-loop escalation.

Outcome: Moves the team toward faster response times, lower alert fatigue, and more scalable operational coverage.

Product Strategy

Visa Dispute Revenue Forecasting

Outcome: Produced a forecast identifying more than $1B in four-year revenue opportunity and earned recognition from the CEO.
